• About Itcham Akak Foundation

    Itcham Akak is a non-profit organization committed to addressing food insecurity and improving education in African countries. We support underprivileged children by supplying food aid and school materials.

  • Our Goals

    The organization's goal is to empower children through nourishment and education, enabling them to flourish.

  • Overview

    It is essential to recognize that hunger is not an isolated issue. This means that we must focus our time, resources, and efforts on the most vulnerable members of our society. It involves not only providing immediate emergency assistance but also implementing tailored multi-year assistance programs that can improve the nutritional status of communities. While taking urgent action to alleviate hunger in the short term is necessary, it is equally important to work toward the goal of eradicating hunger. Confronting global food insecurities requires a multifaceted approach that addresses both immediate and long-term needs. Here are some steps we can take to combat this issue: Increase access to nutritious food Provide emergency aid Implement long-term solutions Address the root causes of hunger fostering partnerships by taking these steps, we can work towards a future where everyone has access to sufficient and nutritious food, and hunger is no longer a global issue.
