Our goal is to improve the nutritional and educational health of disadvantaged children.

Our Foundation's approach is based on cultural sensitivity and respect for diversity. We aim to create strategies that recognize and understand the unique values and beliefs of each country's culture and tribes. By doing so, we can honour and celebrate those traditions while improving each community's nutritional and educational well-being. Our focus is on supporting the youngest members and young pupils aged 3 to 18 in these communities.
